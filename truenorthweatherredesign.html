Project Brief

The project brief provides a way to track progress, identify problems, state solutions, name deliverables, and identify a timeline.

Wireframing

The next step is wireframing. Wireframing lays out the basic design elements and outlines how a webpage will be structured. This step is typically completed without the use of color schemes, typefaces, or other brand elements. The intent is to identify the overall structure of the site and allow for any changes to easily be made prior to advancing to the next steps in the design process.
