Autumn All Year

AutumnAllYear.us is a recipe website designed for followers of the carnivore persuasion. This website is designed with the user experience in mind. It cuts through all the clutter of typical blog-style recipe sites, instead providing a simplified recipe including ingredient lists, instructions, special notes, as well as substitutions and additions It is designed to provide a unified user experience across multiple devices, including desktop, tablet, and mobile.

As an avid cook and photographer by hobby, I carefully crafted these recipes and captured the images. You can access the site by clicking on this text or the accompanying image.

Wok Asian Cuisine

Wok Asian Cuisine was a fictional restaurant site for which I designed the Home, Our Menu, Order Now, and Contact Us pages, as well as a page aimed at educating users about web security. This site was designed with the user experience and accessibility in mind.

JavaScripting was utilized for the calculations on the Order Now page as well as error feedback on the Contact page. Visit the project page by clicking here or on the accompanying image to learn more about the design process.

Two Trees Creative


Two Trees Creative is an advertising website for a fictitious businesse. This site was designed to be simple, while offering clients insight into the design firm's creative team, portfolio, and location, as well as providing a contact form. This site is an example of a single-page, advertising webpage.

Two Trees Creative's website is accessible on mobile and tablet has not been coded for responsive web design. This site is an example of a site designed for desktop/laptop access. A site designed for a live business would always be coded with responsive web design elements in mind, making the site highly accessible across different device platforms.

Autumn's Hand Crafted

Autumn's Hand Crafted is a premium skin care product line for which I created the brand elements, including typefaces, color palette, logo, and images. As an all natural line, Autumn's Hand Crafted needed a brand designed to let the products shine while still being visible and recognizable.

No longer in operation, I am showcasing Autumn's Hand Crafted as an example of small business brand design.
