Autumn All Year

AutumnAllYear.us is a recipe website designed for followers of the carnivore persuasion. This website is designed with the user experience in mind. It cuts through all the clutter of typical blog-style recipe sites, instead providing a simplified recipe including ingredient lists, instructions, special notes, as well as substitutions and additions It is designed to provide a unified user experience across multiple devices, including desktop, tablet and mobile. As an avid cook and photographer by hobby, I have written all the recipes and taken the photos myself. I designed and wrote the HTML and CSS code.
