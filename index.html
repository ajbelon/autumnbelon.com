My Designs

Autumn All Year

AutumnAllYear.us is a recipe website designed with the user experience in mind. It cuts through all the clutter of other blog-style recipe sites, providing a simplified recipe including ingredient lists, instructions, special notes, as well as substitutions and additions. It is designed to provide a unified user experience across multiple device platforms, including desktop, tablet, and mobile.

As an avid cook and photographer by hobby, I carefully crafted these recipes and captured the images.

Wok Asian Cuisine

Wok Asian Cuisine was a fictitious restaurant site for which I designed the Home, Our Menu, Order Now, and Contact Us pages, as well as a page aimed at educating users about Web Security. This site was designed with the user experience and accessibility in mind.

JavaScripting was utilized for the calculations on the Order Now page as well as error feedback on the Contact page. Visit the project page by clicking here or on the accompanying image to learn more about the design process.

Two Trees Creative

Two Trees Creative is a single-page, advertising website for a fictitious business and is designed to be simple, while offering clients insight into the firm's creative team, portfolio, location, and providing a contact form.

While Two Trees Creative is accessible on mobile and tablet, it has not been coded for responsive web design. A site designed for a live business would always be coded with responsive web design elements, making the site highly accessible across device platforms.

Autumn's Hand Crafted

Autumn's Hand Crafted is a premium skin care product line for which I created the brand elements, including typefaces, color palette, logo, and images. As an all natural line, Autumn's Hand Crafted needed a brand designed to let the products shine while still being visible and recognizable.
