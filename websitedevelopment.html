Website Development and Management

The Client:

AutumnAllYear.us - a recipe website

The Ask:

I created this recipe website based upon my own interests. As a foodie, I wanted to create a space where I could share recipes, tips, and blog posts. The main elements I wanted to capture on this website were:

  1. A home/landing page.
  2. Categories including food type and meal type.
  3. Simplified recipe pages.
  4. A place for blog posts.
  5. Visually compelling design and images.
  6. High usability and accessibility.
  7. A Content Management System (CMS) platform.
