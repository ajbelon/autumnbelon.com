About Burrito Brigade

Burrito Brigade is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing meals to those in need while supporting local food communities. They needed a website that communicates their mission clearly and makes it easy for volunteers and donors to engage.

Challenges & Goals

  • Outdated design with poor accessibility and navigation.
  • Complex content that made it difficult for visitors to find information quickly.
  • Goal: Create a user-friendly, accessible site aligned with WCAG standards that drives engagement and simplifies donations.

Problem → Solution

Outdated Design
Redesigned with a clean, modern look and WCAG-aligned accessibility.
Information hidden in accordions
Moved content into visible, scannable sections for faster comprehension.
No events page
Added a dedicated events page to showcase volunteer opportunities and community events.
Accessibility gaps
Applied WCAG 2.1 standards: proper color contrast, semantic markup, and keyboard navigation.
Duplicate donation pages
Combined monetary & in-kind donation pages into one streamlined, easy-to-use page.
Overly verbose
Simplified body copy, used concise language and clear headings
Donation Impact Unclear
Provided meaningful, clear examples of how donations are utilized and the benefit to the community.
No metrics tracking installed
Implemented Google Site Tag to track engagement and conversions.

Results & Impact

  • Improved accessibility scores across multiple pages.
  • Simplified navigation led to a measurable increase in volunteer sign-ups and donations.
  • Clear calls-to-action increased page interactions by 35% within the first 2 months.
  • Positive client feedback on clarity and user-friendliness.
