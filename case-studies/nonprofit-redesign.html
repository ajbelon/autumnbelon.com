Case Study: Burrito Brigade Website Redesign

This case study breaks down how I redesigned Burrito Brigade’s website to remove usability barriers, simplify complex content, and clearly communicate how donations and volunteer efforts support the local community.

About Burrito Brigade

Burrito Brigade is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing meals to those in need while supporting local food communities. They needed a website that communicates their mission clearly and makes it easy for volunteers and donors to engage.

Challenges & Goals

  • Outdated design with poor accessibility and navigation.
  • Complex content that made it difficult for visitors to find information quickly.
  • Goal: Create a user-friendly, accessible site aligned with WCAG standards that drives engagement and simplifies donations.

Problem → Solution

Problem: Outdated Design
Solution: Redesigned with a clean, modern look and WCAG-aligned accessibility.
Problem: Information hidden in accordions
Solution: Moved content into visible, scannable sections for faster comprehension.
Problem: No events page
Solution: Added a dedicated events page to showcase volunteer opportunities and community events.
Problem: Accessibility gaps
Solution: Applied WCAG 2.1 standards: proper color contrast, semantic markup, and keyboard navigation.
Problem: Duplicate donation pages
Solution: Combined monetary & in-kind donation pages into one streamlined, easy-to-use page.
Problem: Overly verbose
Solution: Simplified body copy, used concise language and clear headings
Problem: Donation Impact Unclear
Solution: Provided meaningful, clear examples of how donations are utilized and the benefit to the community.
Problem: No metrics tracking installed
Solution: Implemented Google Site Tag to track donations, volunteer sign-ups, and page interactions.

Results & Impact

  • Improved accessibility scores across multiple pages.
  • Simplified navigation led to a measurable increase in volunteer sign-ups and donations.
  • Clear calls-to-action increased page interactions by 35% within the first 2 months.
  • Positive client feedback on clarity and user-friendliness.
