Waste to Taste

Goal: Educate the community about food rescue initiatives and increase awareness of upcoming events and opportunities.

Strategy: Developed a consistent posting cadence and brand-aligned visuals to explain program operations, communicate needs, and highlight events—humanizing the organization while keeping the audience informed.

Tactics & Approach:

  • Published 3–4 posts per week sharing updates on program operations and community events.
  • Created short-form videos and graphics explaining how the program works and how the community can get involved.
  • Maintained a consistent visual identity to make posts recognizable and trustworthy.
  • Leveraged polls and Q&A sessions to clarify program details and answer public questions.
  • Cross-posted content on Facebook and Instagram to maximize reach and audience consistency.

Results & Lessons Learned:

  • Posts educating the public and explaining program operations drove a 94.5% increase in Facebook views.
  • Consistent updates and clarity on events and needs led to 138.9% net follower growth.
  • Iterative adjustments based on engagement metrics improved reach organically without paid ads.

Little Free Pantry

Goal: Increase visibility and engagement while educating the public on how the pantry operates and how they can participate.

Strategy: Created posts that explained program logistics, promoted events, and highlighted community needs—helping followers understand the impact of their support.

Tactics & Approach:

  • Regular posts explaining how the pantry functions, what donations are needed, and when events occur.
  • Branded visuals for program updates to make posts consistent and recognizable.
  • Used interactive posts like polls to engage the community and answer common questions.
  • Cross-posted on multiple platforms to maintain visibility and awareness.

Results & Lessons Learned:

  • Clear, educational content drove a 331.8% increase in Facebook Views.
  • Posts clarifying program operations and community needs led to 32.3% net follower growth.
  • Strategic content helped the organization increase engagement and visibility without paid promotion.

Burrito Brigade

Goal: Strengthen community understanding, engagement, and awareness of flagship programs through educational content.

Strategy: Implemented a consistent posting strategy and visual identity to communicate program operations, announce events, and showcase how the organization functions—humanizing the brand and keeping followers informed.

Tactics & Approach:

  • Regular updates on program operations and upcoming events.
  • Branded graphics and concise explanations to make content clear and shareable.
  • Interactive posts answering audience questions and providing clarity on how to get involved.
  • Cross-platform posting to ensure consistent messaging and visibility.

Results & Lessons Learned:

  • Educational posts and clear communication led to a 129.5% increase in Facebook Views.
  • Consistent program updates helped maintain engagement and grow net follows over time.
  • Focusing on educating the audience reinforced trust and positioned the organization as transparent and accessible.
