Human-Centered is the main tenet of any well designed project. Whether designing websites, logos, or creating an entire brand strategy, ease of use is the top priority. As a UX/UI web developer and HTML and CSS coder, allowing the visual elements to come together in a way that is intuitive, requiring no explanations, is the goal.

AutumnAllYear.us is a recipe website designed with the user experience in mind. It cuts through all the clutter of other blog-style recipe sites, providing a simplified recipe including ingredient lists, instructions, special notes, as well as substitutions and additions. It is designed to provide a unified user experience across multiple device platforms, including desktop, tablet, and mobile.

As an avid cook and photographer by hobby, I carefully crafted these recipes and captured the images.
