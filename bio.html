My Bio

I earned a Master of Science in Graphic Information Technology, with a concentration in user experience design principles, in May of 2022, and have a Bachelor of Applied Science in Internet and Web Development. I have over 8 years of industry experience with a strong track record of success and a great reputation as someone who delivers on promises. I am transitioning into user experience research and currently seeking a full-time role.

I have a passion for helping others. Prior to embarking on my career in IT, I worked with children who were living in foster care. My role at the foster care agency as an Inclusion Facilitator was to work with children with developmental delays to empower them for success by helping them gain life skills.

I currently volunteer weekly with a non-profit food rescue organization called Waste to Taste. I wrote an article about the organization and my experiences volunteering there, which was published in The Spark, found on page 43.

I love gardening, growing my own food, and just being outdoors. Combining my love for gardening and my passion for helping and teaching others I wrote an article on Vermicompost, which was also published in The Spark, found on page 2.

Examples of my work as a content creator can be found on my TikTok account, Autumn Farms, and Waste to Taste's Instagram Account.

An example of my web design can be found on my recipe website, Autumn All Year.
